About College
The Foundation of Government Polytechnic Sumerpur was laid down in the year 2008. This Institute is Situated on Keeratpur, Sumerpur, District: Hamirpur Uttar Pradesh

Initially this institute started its academic session in year 2008 with only one discipline Mechanical Engineering 66 seats.
