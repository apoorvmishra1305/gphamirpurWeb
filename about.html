ABOUT US
The Government Polytechnic Sumerpur is situated near Industrial Area of district Hamirpur which provides advantage of exposure and opportunities to students of instituteInstitute is situated at calm, serene and pollution free environment of Tapo Bhumi and also at the bank of river Betwa and Yamuna which makes student healthy.Institute is nearly 500 meters from national Highway 62 and hence it is very well connected with all major cities of our state as well as nearby states.

To develop competencies in students by providing them skills, knowledge and attitude to achieve their goals.To develop practicing engineers who investigate, research, design, and find workable solutions to complex engineering problems with awareness and concern for society, and environment.To develop the approach of life long learning and contributing towards the betterment of society by keeping abreast with new cutting edge technologies and human values.To provide learning environment where students can develop technical, managerial interpersonal and administrative skills.


All the courses are approved by both the state Board of Technical Education U.P.(BTEUP) Lucknow and All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE). Diploma Certificate in all the discipline are given by board of Technical Education U.P. Lucknow.
